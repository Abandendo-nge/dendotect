Completed in 2023, Casa Lúmina is a striking modern residence located in the hills outside São Paulo, Brazil.

The house features open-plan living spaces, three bedrooms, a home office, and unique elements such as a cantilevered deck and rooftop garden. Designed to maximize natural light and ventilation, the home incorporates large glass panels, a double-height living room, and seamless indoor-outdoor transitions. The layout and structure respond to the site's southwest orientation and existing oak trees, adhering to zoning regulations and height limits.
